I was born and raised in rural Nova Scotia. After school, I relocated to Ontario for work. I thought I was lucky to find a good government job and what I discovered was a rapidly evolving career in the technology sector. Humble beginnings in technical support acted as stepping stones to more senior roles, including a key stint in IT Security. Then a major government transformation created career acceleration that pulled me into management while still in my 20s. At that point, it was clear I'd found my niche and have been leading teams ever since.

Along the way, I met an American girl on the internet and moved to Southern Ontario to be closer to her. We got married and had two beautiful children. After our daughter arrived, we decided to move back to Nova Scotia to provide a better life for our growing family. A big part of that has been the addition of our two miniature dachshunds, Deebo and Daisy.

What a ride. :vulcan_salute:
