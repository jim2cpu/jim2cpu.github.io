I grew up in rural Nova Scotia. After school, I relocated to Ontario for work. I thought I was lucky to find a good government job and what I discovered was a fulfilling leadership career in the technology sector.

I met a girl and moved south to be closer to her. We got married and had two beautiful children. We decided to move back to Nova Scotia to provide a better life for our family. A big part of that has been the addition of our two miniature dachshunds, Deebo and Daisy.
