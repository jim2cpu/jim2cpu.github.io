After years of toiling away with Home Care Nova Scotia as a Supervisor of Client Services, my mother recently decided to make a change and try something new. She's now the Director of Nursing at the RK MacDonald Nursing Home in Antigonish, Nova Scotia.

I know she's going to do great. She's a smart lady who just happens to be the most organized person I know. Everyone loves her (who wouldn't, just look at that face) so she'll quickly make friends at her new place of work. Thankfully now she won't be travelling around the province as much as she was forced to do in her old job. The early mornings and long hours spent on the roads in her purple Jeep really wore her out.

Congratulations Mom! I love you. :slightly_smiling_face:
