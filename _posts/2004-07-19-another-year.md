Alicia and I celebrated my birthday (and her Father and Brother's) over the weekend. Lots of food was eaten and Alicia bought me some great gifts as always!

My big present will arrive in a couple of weeks when Alicia and I take 9 days off and vacation in Nova Scotia with my family. That will be a lot of fun for both of us.
