New Year's Day seems like the perfect time for a family announcement. We are pleased to share that we have a new addition to the family. Daisy has joined the household as our second furbaby.

We have been considering this for awhile and after a quick check-in with our local miniature dachshund breeder ([Long Long Ranch](https://www.longlongranch.com/)), an unexpected opportunity surfaced. Daisy, a 4-month old pup, had come back to them and needed a new home. They asked us if we would be interested in a trial period with her to see how she fit in with our kiddos and our other doxie, Deebo. Needless to say, after a few days... it was clear that Daisy was here to stay.

We are adjusting to 2x the dog food, 2x the dog poop and 2x the dogs in our bed at night... but it's all good.

Welcome Daisy!
