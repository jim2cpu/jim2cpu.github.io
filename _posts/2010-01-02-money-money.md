Alicia and I just spent an hour or so going over our budget for 2010. We're going to have to make some cut backs in a couple of areas. My net income changes a bit because at the start of the year, the good old [Canada Pension Plan](https://www.canada.ca/en/services/benefits/publicpensions/cpp.html) kicks in again... they can't seem to spread the contributions over the entire year... for me, I pay in from January to July and then it goes away for the remainder of the year... giving us a sense of inflated income.

In addition to that, we're sending Andrew to daycare 2 days per week to get him some needed socialization. It will be expensive, but really good for Drewby.

Alicia and I really enjoy watching the show ["Til Debt Do Us Part"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Til_Debt_Do_Us_Part) on Slice... we think Gail would be proud of how we're communicating about our budget and how well we're tracking our expenses. :)
