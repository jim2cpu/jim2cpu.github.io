Well, the Super Bowl turned out to be rather entertaining last night even though the evil Patriots ended up winning the game 32-29.

I hosted a Super Bowl party for two at my place, as my Dad flew up from Nova Scotia for the weekend's festivities. We watched some movies, did a little bit of shopping, cooked and ate a lot of food and had a great time watching the game. I drove him to the Airport in Hamilton early this morning as he was going to fly back to Halifax through Ottawa.

So now we all have to go back to the daily grind? Boo!
