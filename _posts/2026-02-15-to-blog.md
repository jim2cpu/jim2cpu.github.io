My goodness, jimkirk.org has gone through so many iterations over the decades. You read that right. Decades.

From blogging about my personal life and all those trips to Pennsylvania to see [that girl](https://www.instagram.com/aliciakirk05), to using it as a bit of an outlet during the pandemic and all parts in between. The evidence of this pattern is not hard to find. Just have a look in the [Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/web/20260000000000*/jimkirk.org).

I'm not sure how much I'll write and what I may write about. The urge to share comes and goes as the years pass by.

I will consider using the [Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/web/20260000000000*/jimkirk.org) to manage an archive of every post I've made over the course of the last 20+ years. Seeing that in one archive, one list, might be amusing if nothing else.

Let's see where this goes.
