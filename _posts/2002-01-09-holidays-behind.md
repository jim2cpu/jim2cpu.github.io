It's 2002... isn't that amazing? I hope everyone had a wonderful holiday season with friends and family. I know I did. My trip to Nova Scotia for Christmas went very well. It was great to spend some time with my family in my boyhood home. I always feel comfortable there.

New Years, I spent with Alicia and her family in Pennsylvania. We had a fantastic time. She's a special young lady. Every guy, at some point in his life, should get to ring in the new year with a kiss from a girl he really cares about. Special times.

I'm up to my ears in work again, which is to be expected. I can't complain, as I'm lucky enough to even have a job in this current IT downturn. Let alone one that I enjoy doing.

I'm done for now. We return you to your regularly scheduled programming.

UPDATE: Virtua Tennis for the Dreamcast is quite possibly one of the funnest games I've played in ages. I'm not even talking about the most recent version. I just grabbed this for 16.99 at EB. The graphics are startling for a 1st gen game, and the play is straightforward, and fun for the whole family. Love it.
