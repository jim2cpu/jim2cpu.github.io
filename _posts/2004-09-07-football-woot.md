Ah, it's that time of the year again folks. It's NFL Football time, babay! As you can tell, I'm pretty excited about the upcoming season which will be kicking off on Thursday night.

Hopefully our beloved Pittsburgh Steelers can put it all together and have a better season than last year. Our Offensive Line is relatively healthy, even with the loss of our starting right guard (Kendall Simmons) for the season. We've been running the ball well during the preseason and hopefully that continues next Sunday when the Raiders are in town. We hate the Raiders.

I've signed on for Fantasy Football again this season with a different crew of guys. We threw our own league together over at Yahoo where that sort of thing is accessible free of charge. I had a decent draft. I'm a little thin at Running Back, but I've got some solid talent at QB, Wide Receiver and on Defense. Generally, most leagues favor teams with stud Running Backs, but thanks to a change in the scoring format increasing the value of Receivers (points for individual receptions), I should be just fine. Here's a list of the notables on my roster:

* QB: Matt Hasselbeck
* WR: Torry Holt
* WR: Hines Ward
* RB: Ahman Green
