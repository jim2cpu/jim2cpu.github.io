Who needs an SUV, eh? The IKEA employees standing outside helping other customers load their vehicles looked skeptical but I managed to shoe-horn all of this goodness into my now legendary 2007 Nissan Versa!

This was the result of a rather big trip to IKEA. We bought the following:

* 1x Mikael Desk
* 1x Mikael Desk Add-On
* 1x Groland Kitchen Island
* 1x Expedit Book Case
* 1x Jerker Computer Table

The second Jerker desk will be configured in an upright position for all of my test equipment in our furnace room. It was on sale for 79.99, how could I not buy a second one?
