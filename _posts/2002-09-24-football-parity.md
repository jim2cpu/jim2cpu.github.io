Parity is the buzz word this season in the NFL. There seems to be a lot of balance; any team can win on any particular day (Unless you're wearing a St. Louis Rams jersey).

There have been a lot of shockers thus far what with the Rams sitting at 0-3 and the Steelers lying at 0-2. On the other side of the coin, you have the Carolina Panthers at 3-0. Of course, they haven't really played anyone yet. At least the Bengals still suck. We can always count on them for consistency!

Miami has to be pleased with their start thus far (although they've always been great in September). They haven't just won their three games, they've dominated. Ricky Williams becomes the first dolphin ever to rush for 100+ yards in three consecutive games? His presence in the backfield is also helping Fiedler at quarterback, and he's been solid so far.

I think this could be a fun season... if the Steelers start playing some Defense. Sigh.
