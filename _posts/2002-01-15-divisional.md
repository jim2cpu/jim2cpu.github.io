Alright, I'm 1 and 4. I must improve, so here we go again. First let's list the games on tap for this weekend:

  * Philadelphia at Chicago
  * Oakland at New England
  * Baltimore at Pittsburgh
  * Green Bay at St. Louis

In the first contest, I'm going to take Chicago by a couple of field goals. I'm not sure if Miller can make it happen, but I hope so. Second, I'm going to take Oakland over New England. This is a matter of experience and big game players. Tim Brown probably felt a little 'ignored' last week because he didn't get a lot of touches. I think Gannon will be throwing the ball his way more this week. I say Raiders by a touchdown in this one. Thirdly, you know I'm going to pick the Steelers. I'm slightly concerned as Baltimore is tough (especially on the run), and Bettis might be rusty. I think this will be close, but I'll give the Steelers the edge by a field goal (which in of itself is scary because that field goal will have to be kicked by Kris Brown... *shudder*). Lastly, I'll take St. Louis over the Packers. Green Bay is a good team, and Brett Farve is an amazing big game quarterback, but the Rams are just too good.
